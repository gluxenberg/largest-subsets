Largest Subsets Test

Forensic Analytics - Identify unusually large subset totals
Nigrini's Methodology: This test groups transactions by subset identifier (vendor, branch, etc.), calculates total amounts per subset, then ranks them by size. The largest subsets are identified for investigation based on their ranking, percentage of total, and materiality - not statistical tests.
